Lasagna by Facu

This lasagna its one of my favorites family recipes. This lasagna is so easy to made, that anyone without cooking knowledge can do it.

Ingredients

  1. 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  2. 1 pound lean ground beef
  3. salt and pepper to taste
  4. 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  5. 1 clove garlic, minced
  6. ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  7. ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  8. 1 pint ricotta cheese
Lasagna
